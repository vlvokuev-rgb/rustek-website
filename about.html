About Rustek

A Swiss company that makes EAC compliance simple for foreign suppliers.

Rustek is headquartered in Lugano, Switzerland, and provides a commercially clear route for international clients needing compliance and documentation support for Kazakhstan and selected Central Asian deliveries.

Our focus is imported industrial equipment — certification, technical passports, translations and final project documentation. We specialise in pressure equipment, pipeline valves, pumps, compressor packages, gas-turbine equipment, automation and control equipment, Ex equipment and packaged equipment requiring project-specific documentation.

We support foreign equipment manufacturers and suppliers and EPC contractors throughout the project — from the first tender review to final handover.
