A technical passport is the project-specific document set that accompanies imported equipment.
Imported equipment for Kazakhstan may require an EAC certificate or declaration of conformity, a permit for use, and a technical documentation package depending on the equipment type, TR CU regulation, pressure category, Ex classification and project specification.
Technical passport requirements for equipment in Kazakhstan usually cover equipment identification, manufacturer details, design and operating parameters, materials, inspections, certificates, drawings, manuals and safe operation information accepted by the client, EPC contractor and local authorities.
Yes — the EAC mark is recognised across all EAEU member states.
An authorised representative appointed by the manufacturer is a company or individual entrepreneur registered in the EAEU and appointed by a foreign manufacturer under an agreement to represent its interests in matters of confirming product conformity with TR CU / EAEU technical regulation requirements. This representative may act as the applicant for a certificate or declaration of conformity and assumes the relevant obligations before EAEU control authorities.
