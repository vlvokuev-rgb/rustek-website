The problem

Certification risk shows up too late — usually after award.

Foreign suppliers regularly underestimate EAC and national requirements at bid stage — then face delays, re-testing and documentation gaps that threaten delivery dates and payment milestones.

Rustek removes that uncertainty. We define the likely compliance route early, build the documentation, and carry certification and technical passports through to delivery — so compliance never becomes the reason a project slips.

What we do

A complete documentation package

All services→
01

TR CU / EAEU certificates and declarations

Full assistance with the issuance of certificates and declarations of conformity in accordance with TR CU / EAEU requirements for imported industrial equipment, including support for visits by accredited experts to the manufacturing facility for audits and witnessed testing, as well as provision of local representation services.

02

Permits for use in Kazakhstan

Obtaining permits for use from Kazakhstan's competent authority — the Committee for Industrial Safety and Industrial Development.

03

Technical Passports

Preparation and coordination of technical passports (formulyars) for any equipment, including pressure vessels, pipeline valves, pumps, compressors, instrumentation, packaged units, explosion-proof equipment, automation and control systems, in line with GOST standards and national requirements.

04

Safety Justification

Preparation of safety justifications required under TR CU 032 for pressure equipment and TR CU 010 for machinery and equipment — the engineering case demonstrating safe operation across the equipment's full life cycle.

05

Manual Translation & Adaptation

Translation and adaptation of operation and maintenance manuals to local standards — so the documentation is accepted by the client, the operator and the authorities on site.

06

Local Representative Service for EAEU / TR CU Certification

We provide local representative support where this is required for EAEU / TR CU declarations and certificates. For foreign manufacturers supplying equipment to Kazakhstan and other EAEU markets, a local applicant or representative may be required for certain declarations or certificates, especially if the manufacturer does not have its own office in Kazakhstan or another EAEU member state. We help clients address this requirement as part of the normal certification process.
