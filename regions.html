EAC certification Kazakhstan

For most imported industrial equipment, Kazakhstan conformity assessment starts with identifying the applicable TR CU / EAEU technical regulations. The result may be an EAC certificate, an EAC declaration of conformity, or a combination of documents depending on the equipment category and risk level.

EAEU certification for equipment under TR CU 010, TR CU 012, TR CU 020 and TR CU 032
EAC certification for pressure equipment in Kazakhstan, including vessels, pipelines, fittings and pressure equipment components
Certification for imported equipment in Kazakhstan checked at tender stage before costs and timing are fixed

Technical passport Kazakhstan

A technical passport for equipment in Kazakhstan is part of the project documentation package expected for many industrial deliveries. It records the equipment identity, design parameters, operating limits, materials, inspections and safe-use information required by the client, EPC contractor and local authorities.

Kazakhstan technical passport for equipment prepared in parallel with certification
Kazakhstan project documentation requirements reviewed against the delivery dossier, drawings, manuals and certificates
Certification and technical passports for package equipment in Kazakhstan coordinated as one documentation package

What documents are required to supply equipment to Kazakhstan?

The exact list depends on the equipment type, HS code, pressure or Ex classification, client specification and project stage. Kazakhstan documentation requirements for imported industrial equipment may include an EAC certificate or declaration, technical passport, safety justification, adapted operation manuals, translated drawings and project-specific conformity records.
