Services

Everything between winning the bid and handing over the dossier.

A complete documentation package for imported equipment: certificates, declarations, permits, technical passports, safety justifications and adapted manuals.

01

TR CU / EAEU certificates and declarations

Preparation and coordination of certificates and declarations of conformity under TR CU / EAEU requirements for imported industrial equipment.

TR CU / EAEU conformity route definition
Certificates and declarations by equipment category
Coordination with accredited bodies
Cost and timing assessment at tender stage
02

Permits for use in Kazakhstan

Obtaining permits for use from Kazakhstan's competent authority — the Committee for Industrial Safety and Industrial Development.

Permit applicability check
Submission package preparation
Kazakhstan authority coordination
Integration into the delivery dossier
03

Technical Passports

Preparation and coordination of technical passports (formulyars) for any equipment, including pressure vessels, pipeline valves, pumps, compressors, instrumentation, packaged units, explosion-proof equipment, automation and control systems, in line with GOST standards and national requirements.

Passports (formulyars) for each item
Pressure, Ex, packaged and automated equipment
Prepared to GOST and national requirements
Coordinated with project documentation
04

Safety Justification

Preparation of safety justifications required under TR CU 032 for pressure equipment and TR CU 010 for machinery and equipment as part of the standard documentation package for supplied equipment.

Safety justification for TR CU 032
Safety justification for TR CU 010
Included in certification and declaration dossiers
Prepared from design and operating documentation
05

Manual Translation & Adaptation

Translation and adaptation of operation and maintenance manuals to local standards — so the documentation is accepted by the client, the operator and the authorities on site.

Operation and maintenance manuals
Adaptation to local standards
Technical translation by industry specialists
Format and content compliance
